Welcome from Disney Channel

Disney Junior is a television channel and programming block targeted at preschool-aged children, typically between the ages of 2 and 7. It features a mix of animated and live-action shows, movies, and other content designed to entertain and educate young children. Disney Junior is owned and operated by Disney, and it has expanded globally, often replacing or incorporating content from Playhouse Disney.
